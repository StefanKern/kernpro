# Cover Letter Templates Guide

This document provides an overview of the 7 professional cover letter templates available in the application.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the Cover Letter Editor
2. Use the **Template Style** dropdown in the header
3. Select your preferred template
4. The PDF preview will automatically update
5. Download when ready

---

## Template 1: Classic

**Best for:** Traditional industries, formal positions, conservative companies

**Design Features:**

- Traditional left-aligned layout
- Standard margins (1 inch)
- Black text on white background
- Professional Helvetica font
- Clean, no-nonsense formatting

**Industries:** Finance, Law, Academia, Government, Healthcare

---

## Template 2: Modern Accent

**Best for:** Tech companies, startups, modern corporations

**Design Features:**

- Full-width blue accent bar at the top
- Large, bold applicant name
- Contact info with icon symbols (phone, email, location)
- Thin decorative line separator
- Blue color scheme (#2C5F9C primary, #1E88E5 accent)

**Industries:** Technology, Software Development, Digital Marketing, Design

---

## Template 3: Minimalist

**Best for:** Design-focused roles, creative positions with professional standards

**Design Features:**

- Centered header with applicant name
- Single-line contact info with bullet separators
- Thin horizontal line under header
- Generous white space
- Subtle gray accents (#2C3E50 primary, #7F8C8D accent)
- Slightly smaller font for sophisticated look

**Industries:** Graphic Design, UX/UI Design, Architecture, Marketing

---

## Template 4: Professional Blue

**Best for:** Corporate positions, management roles, business consultancy

**Design Features:**

- Thin top border stripe
- Name in light blue box/highlight
- Contact information in structured grid
- Bold blue divider line (#003366 primary, #0066CC accent)
- Bottom border stripe
- Very structured and organized appearance

**Industries:** Management, Consulting, Corporate Business, Sales

---

## Template 5: Two Column

**Best for:** Modern professionals who want to stand out while remaining professional

**Design Features:**

- Left sidebar (gray background) with contact information
- Main content area for letter body
- Sidebar persists across pages
- Efficient use of space
- Clean separation of contact info and content
- Blue-gray color scheme (#34495E primary, #3498DB accent)

**Industries:** Project Management, Engineering, Data Science, Research

---

## Template 6: Creative Bold

**Best for:** Creative industries, artistic positions, innovative companies

**Design Features:**

- Bold diagonal triangular stripe in top-left corner
- Red/coral color scheme (#E74C3C primary, #C0392B accent)
- Large uppercase name
- Contact info in light-colored bar
- Eye-catching and memorable
- More relaxed and creative tone

**Industries:** Creative Writing, Advertising, Fashion, Entertainment, Media

---

## Template 7: Executive

**Best for:** Senior positions, executive roles, high-level management

**Design Features:**

- Gold accent borders (top and bottom)
- Centered, elegant name with gold decorative line
- Sophisticated styling
- "Respectfully" closing (instead of "Sincerely")
- Refined color palette (#1C1C1C primary, #B8860B gold accent)
- Timeless, prestigious appearance

**Industries:** Executive Leadership, C-Suite Positions, Board Positions, Senior Management

## Quick Selection Guide

### By Industry Type

**Conservative (Finance, Law, Government):**

- Classic
- Professional Blue

**Technology (Software, IT, Startups):**

- Modern Accent
- Two Column

**Creative (Design, Media, Arts):**

- Minimalist
- Creative Bold

**Management (Executive, Corporate):**

- Executive
- Professional Blue

**Versatile (Works for Most):**

- Modern Accent
- Minimalist
- Two Column

---

## Tips for Template Selection

1. **Research the Company Culture**: Look at their website, social media, and job posting style
2. **Match the Industry Standard**: Conservative industries prefer traditional layouts
3. **Consider Your Level**: Executive template for senior roles, Modern/Creative for junior/mid-level
4. **Stand Out Appropriately**: Creative templates help you stand out in creative fields but may hurt in conservative fields
5. **Test Multiple**: Generate PDFs of 2-3 templates and pick your favorite

---

## Technical Details

All templates are generated using jsPDF and support:

- Automatic page breaks
- Text wrapping
- Multiple paragraphs
- Custom contact information
- Dynamic content

Templates are defined in: `apps/kernpro/src/app/services/pdf-generator.service.ts`
